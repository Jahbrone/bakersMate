Directions

Extra ingredients: Yeast, pizza toppings.

  • Mix ingredients together and knead to
    make dough.
  • Cover and let rest until it doubles in size, or over night.
  • Divide into equal portions and roll into balls.
  • Rest again until it rises.
  • Strech/roll/whatever each ball out flat and put on your toppings.
  • Cook 1 at a time at your oven's hottest temperature until ready.
