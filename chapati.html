Directions

Extra ingredients: oil

  • Mix ingredients together and knead to
    make dough.
  • Cover with light coating of oil and rest for at least half an hour.
  • Divide into equal portions and roll into balls.
  • Heat skillet on high heat until very hot.
  • Using rolling pin, roll the dough balls out into chapatis.
  • Cook on each side until looking ready.

    • Tips: Once on pan, press down and make small indents with fingers to help it bubble up. Don't be afraid to flip it multiple times.
