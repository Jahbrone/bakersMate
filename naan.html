Directions

Extra ingredients: Yeast, oil, butter, garlic.

  • Mix ingredients together and knead to
    make dough.
  • Cover with light coating of oil and rest until double in size.
  • Divide into equal portions and roll into balls.
  • Heat skillet on high heat until very hot.
  • Lightly roll out into naan, about 1/2cm thick. Don't press too hard when rolling.
  • Cook on each side until it puffs up and looks ready.
  • Add garlic butter, herbs, or any other toppings you want.
