Directions

Extra ingredients: Herbs, garlic, oats, seeds.

  • Mix flour, salt and water and leave for 1/2 hour.
  • Add starter and mix in until it is evenly spread through dough.
  • Stretch and fold 3 times over 2 hours.
  • Bulk ferment until it is ready.
  • Shape the dough, or pre shape if needed.
  • Score dough and bake for 30 minutes at 215c.
  • Let cool for at least an hour before cutting.
