Directions

Extra ingredients: Yeast, eggwhite, maple syrup, sesame seeds (optional).

  • Mix ingredients together with yeast
    and knead to make dough.
  • Cover and leave until doubled in size.
    Around 1-2hrs.
  • Boil a large pot of water with a squirt of syrup.
  • Divide dough into equal parts and shape into bagels.
  • Boil bagels 1 minute each side
  • Baste with eggwhite and add toppings (salt/sesame, seeds etc)
  • Bake for 20-25 minutes at 220c until golden brown.
